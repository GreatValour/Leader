Features

The Mini MICR reads real-world checks with the highest accuracy in the industry. The Mini MICR’s rugged and durable design will provide years of daily use. With over 1 million check readers installed worldwide, the Mini MICR is in service today with major retailers and financial institutions reading billions of checks annually. Mini MICR offers a range of interface options for easy and flexible connectivity to POS terminals, PCs, cash registers, etc.
