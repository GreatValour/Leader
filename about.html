About Leaders Merchant Services

Leaders Merchant Services is a California-based company providing a variety of services including:

  • Merchant Account Services
  • Online Credit Card Payment Processing
  • Point-of-Sale (POS) Systems, Credit Card and PIN Debit Terminals, including Clover POS, Verifone, First Data, and more
  • Mobile Credit Card Readers and Processing, including Apple Pay, Google Pay & Samsung Pay
  • Merchant Cash Advances & Business Loans

We cater to companies of all sizes for their credit and debit card payment processing needs. We serve, with pride, thousands of merchants in all fifty states. We are one of the fastest growing payment processing service providers of credit and debit card based transactions in the country. Our top rated merchant services, credit card equipment and processors, Point-of-Sale Systems, and credit card readers, including iPad and Android credit card readers enable merchants to process both traditional card-present, or “swiped” transactions, as well as card-not-present, or “keyed” transactions.

For more information call us at 800-220-4143.

Leaders Merchant Services is committed to excellence in all aspects of our merchant services relationships. We offer competitive rates for your credit card processing needs and a wide variety of credit card terminals. Our company has one of the largest Chargeback and Fraud Departments in the industry, providing our clients with the highest level of service and protection for their merchant accounts. We`re here 24/7/365 to ensure merchant satisfaction and rapid problem resolution. Retail, home based, mail-order, or internet; we do it all.
