New clover Flex

Let a Clover™ Flex change your business and life for the better!

It’s everything you need on one, simple device.

Small in size, Clover Flex is built to do business wherever you are. Easy to take with you and easy to hand to customers. They can dip, swipe EMV®, tap, enter a PIN and sign, whether in line, in the aisle, or at the table. Built-in receipt printer and scanner ensures Clover Flex delivers the ultimate in flexibility. And it gets better from there…

Clover Flex lets you checkout where you are. Not where your counter is.

No need to run back and forth from your customer to your counter to process a card, print the receipt, get the customer signature, and adjust the tip. Just do it right there with Clover Flex. Small in size, Clover Flex is built to do business wherever you are. Easy to take with you and easy to hand to customers – they can dip, swipe, tap, enter a PIN and sign, whether in line, in the aisle, or at the table. And its built-in receipt printer and scanner ensures Clover Flex delivers the ultimate in flexibility. It’s ultra-portable, so it’s perfect for checking out in the aisle, at the table and at the counter. Accept credit, debit, EMV chip cards, NFC payments (contactless, like Apple Pay®, Android Pay®, gift cards and cash. Plus, we track your sales in real time and provide detailed reports on device, in the web and on our mobile iOS/Android App.

As your business grows, Clover Flex grows with you. From inventory to employee and customer management, you can run a full POS from the same compact device that takes your payments.

Key Features Include:

  • Signatures on the spot. Clover Flex accepts electronic signatures, and emails and texts receipts
  • Manage your inventory. Far from your standard POS system, Clover Flex features a built in 5 MP camera and barcode scanner, so you can scan items into inventory instantly.
  • Stay connected. Wireless LTE SIM card capability lets you take payments anywhere on the road-or use WiFi if closer to home.
  • Take payments even when your connection is down. Clover will run the payments as soon as you’re back online.
  • Plays well with others: Clover Flex works seamlessly with other Clover hardware, so you can take orders in line or at the table, and close them at the counter on another Clover device.
  • Processor: 1.6GHz Quad Core A7
  • Display” Antimicrobial Corning® Gorilla® Glass; 5 inch HD display with a 1280 x 720 LCD color screen for clear, crisp images
  • Connectivity: Wireless LTE SIM card and WiFi enabled
  • Security: PCI PTS V5.0
  • Memory: 1GB RAM, 8GB ROM
  • Battery Life: Supports up to 8 hours of use for a typical SMB (Lithium-Ion 15.56Wh, 2100mAh @ 7.6V)
  • Built In Features: Receipt printer, 1D/2D barcode scanner / camera
  • Electronic Signature: Accepts electronic signatures on-screen; emails, texts and stores digital receipts
