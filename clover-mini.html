New clover Mini

Let a Clover™ Mini change your business and life for the better!

A compact countertop POS with the guts to go big.

A large touchscreen, intuitive interface, and LTE/Ethernet/WiFi Connectivity all make accepting payments easy. You can even add applications to manage employees and keep track of customers. Or plug in an integrated weight scale or a barcode scanner for a faster checkout.

Accept a wide variety of payment types including: Google Pay® Apple Pay® (and other contactless payment types), Credit, debit & EBT card swipes (MasterCard, Visa, American Express, Discover and more), EMV® (chip + PIN; chip + signature), Gift Cards (physical and virtual).

Key Features Include:

  • Ability to accept multiple credit and debit payment types
  • Manage tips and pre-auth tabs and cards
  • On-screen signature capture
  • Built-in printer
  • Front-facing camera
  • Barcode and QR code reading (using front-facing camera)
  • LTE/Ethernet/Wi-Fi connectivity
  • 7″ Antimicrobial Corning® Gorilla® Glass display screen
